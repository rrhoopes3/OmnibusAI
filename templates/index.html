How It Works

1

Monitor

We automatically check Congress.gov for new omnibus and appropriations bills.

2

Parse

The bill's XML is parsed into its hierarchy: divisions, titles, sections, and dollar amounts.

3

Summarize

AI reads each section and generates plain-English breakdowns with key spending figures.

4

Publish

Breakdowns are published here for free, organized by department and program area.
